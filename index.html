Typly is a clean, distraction-free writing application designed for focus. It saves your work automatically to your browser's local storage, ensuring your progress is always safe.

Features:

  • Distraction-free writing environment
  • Real-time character count
  • Auto-save to browser cache
  • Import and export of `.txt` files
  • Customizable settings (font, colors, width)
